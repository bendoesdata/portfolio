Codevember 2019

In which Ben takes a month-long journey into creative coding.



For the month of November, I wrote one creative sketch with code every single day.

Most are created in p5.js and many are open sourced (code is on my OpenProcessing profile).

Each sketch follows the creative prompt for the day. The title below each sketch represents two pieces of information: the prompt for the day followed by my take on the prompt.

I tried to keep a rough diary of the experience by posting each sketch on Instagram with a detailed comment on what I learned by creating it.
